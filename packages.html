Questions we get before booking

Do you set a minimum trip length?

No fixed minimum, but most itineraries run five to fourteen days — long enough for a planner to build in real slack, short enough to stay unhurried rather than exhausting.

What if I need to change dates after booking?

The Fully Booked and Escorted tiers include one re-plan at no charge. Outside of that window, we charge a flat re-planning fee rather than starting the invoice over.

Can two families travel on one itinerary?

Yes, up to six travellers on a single route. Past that, we usually recommend splitting into two coordinated itineraries so no single day gets too crowded.
